Place your vocabulary word lists here:

  vocab/en.txt  — one English word per line
  vocab/fr.txt  — one French word per line

Recommended sources:

  English (~370k words):
    https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dwyl/english-words/master/words_alpha.txt

  French (Lexique383, ~140k words):
    http://www.lexique.org/databases/Lexique383/Lexique383.tsv
    (extract the "ortho" column, one word per line)

The generator filters to words between 3–14 characters automatically.
Without these files, the generator falls back to a tiny built-in demo vocab.
